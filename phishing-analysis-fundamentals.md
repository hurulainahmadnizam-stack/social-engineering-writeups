## 1. Objective
The objective of this exercise is to understand the structure of phishing emails and identify common indicators used in social engineering attacks.

- Platform: TryHackMe
- Exercise Name: Phishing Analysis Fundamentals

## 3. Phishing Indicators Identified
- Suspicious sender email address
- Generic greeting
- Urgent message tone
- Embedded suspicious links

## 4. Social Engineering Techniques Used
- Urgency
- Authority
- Fear

## 6. Impact if Victim Falls for the Attack
If a victim interacts with the phishing email, attackers may gain access to credentials, personal information, or install malware.

## 7. Mitigation & Prevention
- Verify sender email addresses
- Avoid clicking unknown links
- Use email filtering
- Security awareness training

## 8. Conclusion
This exercise highlights how phishing emails exploit human behavior and why awareness is crucial to prevent attacks.
