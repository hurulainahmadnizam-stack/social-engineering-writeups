## 1. Objective
The objective of this exercise is to identify phishing emails and differentiate them from legitimate emails by analysing various indicators.

## 2. Exercise Platform
- Platform: TryHackMe
- Exercise Name: The Phishing Pond

## 3. Phishing Indicators Identified
- Suspicious and misleading URLs
- Urgent language requesting immediate action
- Unusual sender email address
- Attempts to trick users into clicking links

## 4. Social Engineering Techniques Used
- Urgency
- Fear
- Curiosity

## 5. Evidence
(Insert screenshots of phishing email and malicious links)

## 6. Impact if Victim Falls for the Attack
If the victim interacts with the phishing email, attackers may steal credentials, gain unauthorized access, or cause financial loss.

## 7. Mitigation & Prevention
- Carefully inspect email sender addresses
- Do not click suspicious links
- Use multi-factor authentication
- Increase user awareness training

## 8. Conclusion
This exercise demonstrates how phishing attacks exploit human behaviour and highlights the importance of analysing emails before taking action.
